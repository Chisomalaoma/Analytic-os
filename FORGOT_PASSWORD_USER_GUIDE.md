# Forgot Password - User Guide

## How to Reset Your Password

### Step 1: Access Forgot Password
1. Go to the sign-in page
2. Click the **"Forgot password?"** link below the password field

### Step 2: Enter Your Email
1. Enter the email address associated with your account
2. Click **"Send Reset Code"**
3. Check your email inbox for a 6-digit verification code

### Step 3: Verify Your Identity
1. Enter the 6-digit code from your email
2. Click **"Verify Code"**
3. If you didn't receive the code, click **"Resend"**

### Step 4: Create New Password
1. Enter your new password
2. Confirm your new password
3. Ensure your password meets all requirements:
   - ✓ At least 8 characters
   - ✓ One uppercase letter (A-Z)
   - ✓ One lowercase letter (a-z)
   - ✓ One number (0-9)
   - ✓ One symbol (!@#$%^&*...)
4. Click **"Reset Password"**

### Step 5: Sign In
1. You'll be redirected to the sign-in page
2. Sign in with your email and new password

## Password Requirements

Your password must include:
- **Minimum 8 characters** - Make it long enough
- **Uppercase letter** - At least one (A-Z)
- **Lowercase letter** - At least one (a-z)
- **Number** - At least one (0-9)
- **Symbol** - At least one (!@#$%^&*(),.?":{}|<>_-+=[]\/`~)

### Good Password Examples
- `MyP@ssw0rd!`
- `Secure#2024`
- `Tr0ng!Pass`
- `W3lc0me@Home`

### Bad Password Examples
- `password` - No uppercase, number, or symbol
- `Password` - No number or symbol
- `Pass123` - Too short, no symbol
- `PASSWORD123` - No lowercase or symbol

## Troubleshooting

### I didn't receive the verification code
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Wait 60 seconds and click "Resend"
- Verify you entered the correct email address

### My verification code expired
- Codes expire after 10 minutes
- Request a new code by clicking "Resend"

### My password doesn't meet requirements
- Check the real-time validation indicators
- All checkmarks must be green before you can submit
- Make sure passwords match

### I can't remember my email
- Contact support for assistance
- Check your previous login emails

## Security Tips

1. **Use a strong, unique password** - Don't reuse passwords from other sites
2. **Don't share your password** - Keep it confidential
3. **Change it regularly** - Update your password periodically
4. **Use a password manager** - Consider using a password manager to generate and store strong passwords
5. **Enable notifications** - You'll receive an email when your password is changed
